▶️ Promise.all()

Promise.all is great for when you want to wait for all of the promises passed to resolve. 

When using Promise.all it's important to remember that the promise it returns won't resolve until either: ⏬ 2/10

1️⃣ They all fulfill.
2️⃣ One promise rejects.
